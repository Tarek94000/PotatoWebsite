Types of Potatoes

Russet Potatoes

Great for baking and frying, russet potatoes are starchy and ideal for fluffy mashed potatoes.

Red Potatoes

With their waxy texture, red potatoes hold their shape well, making them perfect for boiling and slicing in salads.

Yukon Gold Potatoes

Yukon Golds are all-purpose potatoes that are just right for roasting, mashing, and making gratins.

Fingerling Potatoes

Small, elongated fingerlings have a waxy texture and are great for roasting or boiling whole.

Purple Potatoes

Purple potatoes have a firm texture and are rich in antioxidants, perfect for roasting or making vibrant potato salads.

White Potatoes

White potatoes are all-purpose potatoes with a thin skin, suitable for mashing, roasting, or boiling.

New Potatoes

New potatoes are young, small potatoes harvested early, perfect for boiling and using in salads.

Jersey Royal Potatoes

Jersey Royals are a type of new potato with a unique flavor, great for boiling and serving with butter and mint.

Sweet Potatoes

Sweet potatoes are versatile and can be roasted, mashed, or used in soups and desserts for a sweet and savory flavor.
