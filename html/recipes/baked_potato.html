Classic Baked Potato

Servings: 4 people

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 1 hour

The perfect baked potato is crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside. This classic recipe ensures that your baked potatoes are a hit, whether served as a side dish or a main course with your favorite toppings.

Ingredient Amount
Russet potatoes 4 large
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Salt 1 teaspoon
  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Scrub the potatoes thoroughly under running water and pat them dry with a towel.
  3. Pierce each potato several times with a fork.
  4. Rub the potatoes with olive oil and sprinkle them with salt.
  5. Place the potatoes directly on the oven rack and bake for 45-60 minutes, or until the skins are crispy and a fork easily pierces the potatoes.
  6. Remove the potatoes from the oven and let them cool slightly.
  7. Cut a slit in the top of each potato and fluff the insides with a fork. Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
  • Grilled steak
  • Chicken breast
  • Steamed vegetables
  • Sour cream, cheese, and chives

Did you know? A baked potato can generate enough electricity to light up a small LED bulb! This is due to the potato's natural electrolytes, which can conduct electricity when paired with metal electrodes. So, next time you bake a potato, just think – you’ve got a delicious snack and a tiny power plant all in one!
