Classic French Fries

Servings: 4 people

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

There's nothing quite like a batch of homemade French fries. Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, these fries are perfect as a side dish or a snack.

Ingredient Amount
Potatoes (Russet) 4 large
Vegetable oil For frying
Salt To taste
  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin strips.
  2. Place the potato strips in a bowl of cold water and let them soak for at least 30 minutes. This helps to remove excess starch and makes the fries crispier.
  3. Drain the potatoes and pat them dry with a clean towel.
  4. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350째F (175째C).
  5. Fry the potatoes in batches for about 3-4 minutes until they are pale and soft. Remove them with a slotted spoon and let them drain on paper towels.
  6. Increase the oil temperature to 375째F (190째C). Fry the potatoes again in batches for another 2-3 minutes until they are golden and crispy.
  7. Remove the fries with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Season with salt while they are still hot.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
